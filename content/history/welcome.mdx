This section will collect the stories of the land at **865 N German Church Rd**,
the people who tend it, and the practices we are bringing back to the soil.

## Why "Rooted Legacy"

Roots and legacy point at the same idea from different sides. Roots are what
holds a plant — or a community — in place. Legacy is what is passed forward
when those roots have done their work.

## What you'll find here

Over time this page will gather:

- Origin stories of the farm and our partners.
- Profiles of growers, teachers, and neighbors.
- Notes on the techniques we use — composting, season extension, native
  plantings, and more.
- Photo essays from events on the land.

## Get involved

Classes, events, and a produce menu are coming. For now, the best way to follow
along is to come visit on a class day. Watch this space.
